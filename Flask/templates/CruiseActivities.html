Free Fall and the Whip

Twist. Shout. Then repeat as often as you like. Exclusively on our Breakaway Class ships is where you'll find five multi-story waterslides. Take the plunge on Free Fall and drop at the speed of 4Gs. Feel the rush of The Whip, two racing side-by-side twister slides that are wet and wild. Or try the family fun slide if that's more your speed.
